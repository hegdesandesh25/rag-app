```
ACME Corporation Leave Policy 2024

Annual Leave:
All full-time employees are entitled to 25 days annual leave per year.
Leave must be approved by your line manager at least 2 weeks in advance.
Unused leave can be carried over up to a maximum of 5 days per year.
Leave during peak periods (December 15 to January 5) requires 4 weeks notice.

Sick Leave:
Employees are entitled to 10 days paid sick leave per year.
A medical certificate is required for absences longer than 3 consecutive days.
Sick leave does not carry over to the following year.

Remote Work Policy:
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week.
Remote work must be agreed with your line manager.
Core hours of 10am to 3pm must be maintained regardless of location.
```
