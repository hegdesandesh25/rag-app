ACME Engineering Team Handbook

On-Call Rotation:
The on-call rotation runs Monday to Monday.
Each engineer is on-call approximately once every 6 weeks.
On-call engineers must respond to P1 incidents within 15 minutes.
On-call compensation is an additional 10% of weekly salary.

Deployment Process:
All deployments must go through the staging environment first.
Production deployments require approval from at least one senior engineer.
Deployments are frozen every Friday at 3pm until Monday morning.
Emergency hotfixes require approval from the engineering manager.

Code Review Standards:
All pull requests require at least 2 approvals before merging.
Pull requests should not exceed 400 lines of code changes.
Reviewers must respond within 24 hours during business days.```
